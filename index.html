PATELINO

STUDIO.

Patelino was born in Rusumo, a small village on the border of Rwanda and Burundi. He grew up surrounded by the natural world, and his art is heavily influenced by his childhood experiences. Patelino's paintings often feature lush forests, rolling hills, and flowing rivers. He is also drawn to the human figure, and his paintings often depict people interacting with the natural world.

Patelino's studio is located in a converted barn on the outskirts of Rusumo. The studio is filled with natural light, and it offers Patelino a space to create art that is both inspired by and connected to the natural world. Patelino's paintings are created using a variety of mediums, including acrylic, oil, and ink. He is known for his meticulous attention to detail and his use of bold colors.
